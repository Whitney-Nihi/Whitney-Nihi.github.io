Nigeria is a country that is very rich in culture and as such, our food are not left out as they are very nutritious and delicious. Here, we have recipes for differnt types of foods from the 3 major ethnic groups in Nigeria - Yoruba, Igbo, and Hausa.

    Nigerian cuisine consists of dishes or food items from the hundreds of ethnic groups that comprise Nigeria.Like other West African cuisines, it uses spices and herbs with palm or groundnut oil to create deeply flavored sauces and soups.Nigerian feasts can be colourful and lavish, while aromatic market and roadside snacks cooked on barbecues or fried in oil are in abundance and varied. Bushmeat is also consumed in Nigeria. The brush-tailed porcupine and cane rats are the most popular bushmeat species in Nigeria. Nigerian cuisine, like many West African cuisines, is known for being spicy.
